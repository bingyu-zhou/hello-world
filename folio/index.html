Bingyu Zhou is a recent communication design graduate of Monash University who keen on animation, illustration and identity design.










THERE IS NO PERFECT BODY

This project aims to uncover the truth that there is no perfect body and to avoid championing the stereotypical ideal body. Let people have a better understanding of what is body shaming and give them some advice about how to deal with it when they are experiencing body shaming.
